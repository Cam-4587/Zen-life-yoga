Kripalu yoga

Kripalu yoga teaches practitioners to understand and learn from the body, focusing on inward practice through breathing exercises, gentle stretches, individual poses, and final relaxation.


Lyengar yoga

This type of yoga practice focuses on finding the correct alignment in each pose with the help of a range of props, such as blocks, blankets, straps, chairs, and bolsters.


Hatha yoga

Hatha yoga is a type of physical yoga that focuses on preparing the body and mind through gentle introductions to basic poses, serving as a comprehensive introduction to yoga.


Kundalini yoga

Kundalini yoga is a meditation technique that aims to release pent-up energy through chanting, singing, asana, pranayama, and meditation, with the goal of achieving a specific outcome.


Ashtanga yoga

Ashtanga is a popular yoga practice that utilizes ancient teachings and focuses on linking movement to breath through specific poses and sequences.


Restorative yoga

Restorative yoga involves a class of four or five simple poses, using props like blankets and bolsters to facilitate deep relaxation without exerting any effort while holding the pose.


Yin yoga

Yin yoga emphasizes long-term passive poses, targeting deep tissues, ligaments, joints, bones, and fascia.
